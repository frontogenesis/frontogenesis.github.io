Southwest Louisiana: For Southwest Louisiana, expect calm marine conditions near the coast today as high pressure settles over the region, with offshore winds and seas gradually decreasing. Close to the coast, temperatures will warm from the 60s on Wednesday to the mid-70s or lower 80s by Friday, with a small chance of showers on Friday. A brief cool-down may follow over the weekend, but overall, warmer-than-average conditions are anticipated into early next week.

Texas Coastal Bend: For the Texas Coastal Bend, expect mild marine conditions with a gentle breeze on Wednesday, shifting to onshore winds by afternoon. Winds will strengthen to a moderate to fresh breeze by Thursday night, then ease by Friday night before a weak cold front arrives on Saturday. Sea fog is possible near the coast and over bays by the end of the week. Along the coast, temperatures will warm gradually, reaching the mid-80s by Friday, with highs in the upper 70s to lower 80s through early next week. No significant precipitation is expected during this period.

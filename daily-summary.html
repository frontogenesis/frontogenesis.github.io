Temperatures will remain warm initially but turn much cooler by Friday into the weekend, with highs dropping to the mid to upper 70s near the coast.

Texas Coastal Bend: For the Texas Coastal Bend, expect gentle to moderate onshore winds through the end of the week, becoming stronger and shifting to the northeast on Friday as a cold front moves through. There’s a low to medium chance (30-60%) of showers and thunderstorms over the coastal waters starting Friday and continuing into the weekend. Near the coast, temperatures will be warm in the 80s to mid-90s on Thursday, cooling to the 70s over the weekend after the front passes.

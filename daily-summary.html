Texas Coastal Bend: For the Texas Coastal Bend, expect gentle to moderate south-southeasterly winds over the weekend, with seas ranging from 3 to 5 feet. Rainfall chances are low to medium, with most areas seeing light accumulations of 0.25 to 1 inch.

Southwest Louisiana: For Southwest Louisiana, especially near the coast, expect overcast skies and periods of heavy rain along with isolated thunderstorms this weekend. Texas Coastal Bend: For the Texas Coastal Bend, expect light to moderate east-southeasterly breezes over the marine areas through the weekend, with occasional gusts. There’s a 30-50% chance of showers and thunderstorms, especially Friday night into Saturday morning, which could affect coastal and marine conditions. Near the coast, temperatures will rise into the 80s on Friday, with a medium to high chance (40-70%) of showers and thunderstorms from Friday afternoon through Saturday morning.

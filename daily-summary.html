Forecast Summary

Updated March 13, 2026

Southwest Louisiana: For Southwest Louisiana, expect pleasant weather near the coast today and tomorrow with temperatures warming into the low to mid-70s today and mid to upper 70s on Saturday under sunny skies. Southerly winds will pick up lightly to moderately over the coastal waters through the weekend. However, a cold front arriving Sunday night will bring showers and storms to the area, along with strong Gale force winds over the marine zones following the front. Be prepared for rough seas and hazardous conditions on the water late Sunday into Monday.

Texas Coastal Bend: For the Texas Coastal Bend, expect pleasant weather near the coast this weekend with highs in the upper 70s to low 80s, while inland areas will be much hotter. Southeasterly winds will be gentle to moderate through Saturday afternoon, with seas around 2-3 feet, but winds will strengthen to fresh levels by Saturday night, increasing seas to 4-5 feet. A strong cold front arriving late Sunday night into early Monday will bring gale conditions for mariners, with very rough seas of 11-16 feet in offshore waters. Conditions should improve by Tuesday morning. Temperatures will drop significantly after the front, with coastal areas seeing highs in the 60s on Monday.

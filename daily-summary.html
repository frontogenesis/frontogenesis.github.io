Southwest Louisiana: For Southwest Louisiana, especially near the coast, expect challenging marine conditions today with strong offshore winds prompting a Gale Warning through midmorning, followed by a Small Craft Advisory until evening. Texas Coastal Bend: For the Texas Coastal Bend, expect challenging marine conditions this morning with gale-force winds and high seas of 8-11 feet, possibly reaching 13 feet further offshore. These conditions will improve by tonight, with winds decreasing and seas dropping to 2-4 feet, continuing calmly through Saturday morning. Another round of near-gale conditions is anticipated Monday morning into the afternoon. Close to the coast, breezy winds are expected this morning, but temperatures will remain mild, with coastal lows in the 50s tonight. Temperatures will climb through the weekend before a cold front arrives late Sunday night, bringing cooler weather into early next week.

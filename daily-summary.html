Southwest Louisiana: For Southwest Louisiana, expect challenging marine conditions near the coast as a cold front moves through early Monday. Strong, gusty winds with sustained speeds of 25-35 knots and gusts up to 40 knots are forecast for late Monday night through Tuesday evening over the Gulf waters, prompting a Gale Warning followed by a Small Craft Advisory. Skies will clear, and no significant precipitation is expected near the coast during this time.

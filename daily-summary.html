Southwest Louisiana: For Southwest Louisiana, expect calm marine conditions near the coast with light to moderate onshore winds and low seas through the week and into the weekend. Texas Coastal Bend: For the Texas Coastal Bend, expect light to gentle southeast winds over the coastal waters through Thursday, increasing to a moderate breeze Thursday night into Friday. Dry weather will dominate near the coast with very slim chances of rain through Saturday, though a slight chance of rain may return Sunday over the Gulf waters. Temperatures will be near normal on Tuesday, warming to above normal starting Wednesday and lasting through the weekend.

Forecast Summary

Updated November 20, 2025

Southwest Louisiana: For Southwest Louisiana, expect light to moderate onshore winds and seas of 1 to 4 feet along the coast through the weekend and into early next week. Near the shore, a Marine Dense Fog Advisory is in effect for coastal lakes and bays until mid-morning today, with potential for more fog tomorrow due to lingering moisture. Rain chances will increase from Friday through early next week, with periods of showers and possible storms. Temperatures will remain well above average, accompanied by humid conditions, through the weekend and at least into the first half of next week.

Texas Coastal Bend: For the Texas Coastal Bend, expect moderate to occasionally strong southeasterly winds over the next few days, with seas ranging from 3 to 5 feet. These winds are forecast to strengthen early next week ahead of an approaching front. Isolated to scattered showers and thunderstorms are possible through the weekend near the coast. Temperatures will remain warmer than normal, with highs in the 80s and lows in the upper 60s to low 70s, though rain chances are generally low along the immediate coast, with slightly higher chances inland.
